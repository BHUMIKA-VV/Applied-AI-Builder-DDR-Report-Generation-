EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
-----------------
A full condition inspection was carried out on the above commercial premises spanning
approximately 3,200 sq ft across a single floor. Several structural and water-related
defects were identified during the inspection. Immediate remedial action is recommended
for two areas; the remaining issues require attention within 90 days.

====================================================
SECTION 1: ROOF / CEILING AREA
====================================================
Observation 1.1 – Active Water Ingress (Main Hall Ceiling)
  Location  : Central ceiling above main hall, approx. 4m x 2m patch
  Description: Visible brown staining and damp patches observed on the false ceiling
               tiles. Two tiles have partially collapsed. Water drip marks visible on
               the floor directly below after recent rainfall.
  Severity   : HIGH
  Photo Ref  : IMG_001.jpg, IMG_002.jpg

Observation 1.2 – Peeling Paint and Efflorescence (East Corridor Ceiling)
  Location  : East corridor, 12m run
  Description: Paint delamination and white mineral deposits (efflorescence) observed
               along the entire length of the east corridor ceiling. Indicates persistent
               moisture migration through the roof slab above.
  Severity   : MEDIUM
  Photo Ref  : IMG_003.jpg

====================================================
SECTION 2: EXTERNAL WALLS
====================================================
Observation 2.1 – Hairline and Structural Cracks (North Façade)
  Location  : North external wall, ground to window level
  Description: A series of diagonal hairline cracks observed running from the window
               corners downward. One crack measures approximately 3mm in width and
               may indicate differential settlement. Render is hollow in two locations
               (confirmed by tapping test).
  Severity   : HIGH
  Photo Ref  : IMG_004.jpg, IMG_005.jpg

Observation 2.2 – Spalling Concrete (South-West Corner Parapet)
  Location  : South-west corner parapet wall
  Description: Concrete spalling with exposed and corroding reinforcement bars visible.
               Carbonation of concrete suspected. Approximately 0.4 sq m area affected.
  Severity   : HIGH
  Photo Ref  : IMG_006.jpg

====================================================
SECTION 3: INTERNAL WALLS
====================================================
Observation 3.1 – Rising Damp (Server Room, East Wall)
  Location  : Server room, lower 600mm of east-facing internal wall
  Description: Dark damp tide mark rising to approximately 600mm from floor level.
               Paint blistering and plaster delamination observed. Damp meter reading
               of 78% (threshold: 20%). No damp proof course (DPC) visible or effective.
  Severity   : CRITICAL
  Photo Ref  : IMG_007.jpg, IMG_008.jpg

Observation 3.2 – Mould Growth (Storage Room B)
  Location  : Storage Room B, north-west corner
  Description: Black mould (likely Cladosporium sp.) identified on internal wall corner.
               Area approximately 0.6m x 0.8m. Likely caused by cold bridging and
               condensation. Room has no mechanical ventilation.
  Severity   : MEDIUM
  Photo Ref  : IMG_009.jpg

====================================================
SECTION 4: FLOORING
====================================================
Observation 4.1 – Hollow and Cracked Tiles (Reception Area)
  Location  : Reception area, approximately 15 sq m of tiled floor
  Description: Hollow sound when tapped across approx. 40% of tiled floor in the
               reception area, indicating loss of adhesion. Three tiles are cracked
               through. No lippage or trip hazard currently, but progressive failure
               likely.
  Severity   : LOW
  Photo Ref  : IMG_010.jpg

====================================================
SECTION 5: ELECTRICAL & MECHANICAL (VISUAL ONLY)
====================================================
Observation 5.1 – Exposed Wiring (IT Room)
  Location  : IT room, above suspended ceiling void
  Description: Several cable runs were observed with damaged insulation and cables
               draped loosely across the ceiling void without containment. Fire risk.
  Severity   : HIGH
  Photo Ref  : IMG_011.jpg

Observation 5.2 – HVAC Unit Leak (Server Room Ceiling)
  Location  : Server room, ceiling-mounted HVAC unit
  Description: Water staining observed below HVAC unit. Condensate drain appears
               partially blocked. Contributing to damp issue on east wall (Obs 3.1).
  Severity   : HIGH
  Photo Ref  : IMG_012.jpg
